Bun Bun Bake Lab: Our Story

Bun Bun started when 3 Italian Americans came together to bring Pittsburgh “traditional” Italian sticky buns. Looking for a way to cash in on a score we realized the value of Italian baked goods. Going from just a pile of cash — all the way to our flagship store on Forbes ave in 3 years — our journey has been magical! We hope that you love our Italian baked goods and look forward to serving you any time. Please ask us about any neighborhood protection services as well - Buona giornata!
