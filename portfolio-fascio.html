Fascio Lighting Collection

Project

The Fascio line was a product line launched in the spring of 2019 that was a collaboration effort between Visual Comfort and Rottet Studio. I began working on this product line in the fall of 2017 with Lauren Rottet and continued during my entire employment with the company. The series was inspired by vintage lighting of the 1920s deco period and the concept of light diffusion through octagonal rods. The product has proved to be a success and was nominated for best lighting product of the year by Interior Design magazine in 2019.

Project

Prototypes of the renders and drawings began to be produced in the early part of 2018. These are photos from the early photo shoots showcasing the product. Some of these materials were later adopted for advertisement and sales usage.

Product Showcasing

By the end of 2018, the Fascio collection began to make its appearance around the country throughout furniture events. This is a photo of the line displayed at BDNY from 2019. Today, the product line is currently available through a number of distrusters world wide.
